Supported Browsers

Internet Explorer and and Legacy Edge(version 44 or below) are not supported.

The following browsers are supported:

  • Firefox (current version)
  • Chrome (current version)
  • Edge (version 88.0 or above)
* Most Chromium-based browsers will work, but are not officially supported.
